EXANINSA LLC provides comprehensive personal insurance solutions designed to protect what matters most to you and your family. Our personalized approach ensures you get exactly the coverage you need at competitive rates.

Auto Insurance:

Customizable protection for all your vehicles with options including:

  • Liability coverage (meets state requirements)
  • Collision and comprehensive protection
  • Uninsured/underinsured motorist coverage
  • Roadside assistance and rental reimbursement
  • Multi-car and safe driver discounts

Homeowners/Renters Insurance:

Complete protection for your residence and belongings:

  • Dwelling and personal property coverage
  • Liability protection for accidents on your property
  • Additional living expenses if displaced
  • Special coverage for high-value items
  • Flexible deductibles to match your budget
 

Umbrella Insurance:

Extra liability protection that extends beyond your auto and home policies to safeguard your assets against major claims.

 

Recreational Vehicle Insurance:

Specialized coverage for motorcycles, boats, RVs, ATVs, and other recreational vehicles with tailored protection options.

 

Life Insurance:

Financial security for your loved ones with various options:

  • Term life (affordable temporary coverage)
  • Whole life (permanent protection with cash value)
  • Universal life (flexible permanent coverage)
  • Final expense insurance
