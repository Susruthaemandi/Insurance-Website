Our Story & Commitment

EXANINSA LLC: Redefining Insurance Excellence Since 2018

In 2018, insurance industry veteran Louis Diez recognized a growing need for personalized, transparent insurance solutions that truly protect what matters most. Founded on this vision, EXANINSA LLC has grown from a local boutique agency to a respected regional leader, now serving clients across 12 states.

What began as a small team of dedicated professionals has flourished into a full-service insurance firm known for its client-first philosophy. Unlike traditional agencies, we combine the expertise of our 20+ year veterans with innovative approaches to risk management, creating tailored protection plans for businesses and individuals alike.

Our Mission

At EXANINSA LLC, we exist to simplify the complex world of insurance while providing exceptional protection tailored to each client's unique circumstances. We go beyond policies to build lasting relationships through:

  • Transparent communication and honest advice
  • Custom solutions rather than one-size-fits-all packages

  • 24/7 claims support when you need it most

  • Integrity: We maintain the highest ethical standards, providing honest assessments and recommendations—even when it means recommending less coverage.
